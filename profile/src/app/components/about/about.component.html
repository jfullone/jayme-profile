About Me

After years of working in healthcare as a physical therapist assistant the COVID-19 pandemic left me furloughed. I took this opportunity to take the leap into the tech world and make the career change that I had been dreaming up. I thrive where I can be creative but also have the ability to be highly analytical so I enrolled in the web development bootcamp with Grand Circus. The six month program helped me to develop not only my technical skills and programming knowledge, but also my ability to work in a team environment.

Throughout my career I have developed and used many skills that will help me on this new path such as leadership, communication, problem-solving, adaptability, and the ability to learn quickly. I am looking for a position that will allow me to contribute these skills to an organization while providing the opportunity to continue developing my technical skills.

Prior to the pandemic my wife and I chose to explore entrepreneurship and launched our side hustle, The Roasted Lotus. The Roasted Lotus is a food truck serving crafted coffee drinks and allergen-friendly baked goods to west Michigan. We dove in to starting this business with no prior knowledge of food trucks and built out the business and the truck from scratch. We sourced our own products and equipment, designed and built up the layout, plumbing, and energy sources, and ran through all of the documentation ourselves. This project required a great deal of learning and pivoting on the fly, attributes that I will bring into the next phase of my career.

When I'm not busy coding and slinging coffee I enjoy time with my two dogs, building and refinishing furniture, and tackling projects around our house in Rockford, Michigan.

If you would like to learn more about what I can contribute to your company and think I could be a fit for your organization please contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
